About us

Hyder Investments and YSH LLC. are two franchisees of McDonald's™ Corporation. Ever since we opened our first McDonald's™ location in Crystal, MN, we have been commited to fulfilling the high standards that McDonald's™ is known for. With a team of over 700 current employees, Hyder Investments and YSH LLCs are continually expanding as McDonald's™ grows.We are excited to welcome new members to our large family and look forward to hiring more dedicated staff.

Hyder Investments Shahab Hyder - CEO

Starting in 1991, Shahab Hyder, owner of Hyder Investments, acquired his first McDonald's™ store in Crystal, MN. With the continual success of this location via hard-working staff and booming business, Hyder Investments gained many additional McDonald's™ restaurants in the Minneapolis Metro area. By 2014, Hyder Investments owned 10 locations in Minnesota. These locations were Crystal, Champlin, Buck Hill, Edinburgh, Medicine Lake, Sycamore Lane (Maple Grove), Osseo, West Broadway (Brooklyn Park), Zane (Brooklyn Park) and Plymouth. With 27 years of experience behind its belt, Hyder Investments has become one of the leading franchisees of McDonald's™ in Minnesota.

YSH LLC. YASmin Hyder - CEO

YSH LLC. is another leading franchisee of McDonald's™ that began in April of 2013. Founded by Yasmin Hyder, the daughter of Shahab Hyder, YSH LLC. has quickly grown in the amount of locations it owns. Starting with the acquisition of Buck Hill and Plymouth from Hyder Investments respectively, YSH LLC. also recently claimed ownership to McDonald's™ locations in New Hope, Wayzata, Minneapolis, Weaver Lake (Maple Grove), Chanhassesn, Waconia, and Norwood. With 9 concurrent locations to maintain, YSH LLC. works dilgently to provide the best McDonald's™ experience to every customer by staffing each location with dedicated and motivated staff.




Shining The Golden Arches

Ever since its beginnings in 1991, Hyder Investments and YSH LLC. have been passionately, 'Shining the golden arches.' In doing so, our companies and staff have received numerous awards for outstanding service. Each of our stores have thier own set of unique awards achieved from their commitment to our customers. No matter which location it is, our goal is the same: Provide the best experience a customer can get. Of course, none of this would be possible without a family of hard-working and driven staff. That being said, we give our sincerest thanks to all the employees who have put forth such an effort day in and day out. Without our great staff, these awards and our continued success would not be possible!
