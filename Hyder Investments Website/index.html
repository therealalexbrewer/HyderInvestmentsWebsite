What is Hyder Invesments and YSH LLC?

Hyder Investments and YSH LLC. are two Owner/Operator run franchisees of McDonald's™ Corporation. Hyder Investments was founded in 1991 by Shahab Hyder and began operations in Crystal, MN. Ever since, Hyder Investments has been dedicated to providing its customers with the best McDonald's™ experience possible. With 8 locations, our company is passionately serving customers throughout the Metro area. YSH LLC. is a joint operator that has run alongside Hyder Investments since 2013. Like Hyder Investments, YSH LLC. is commited to its employees and customers. With 9 unique locations, YSH LLC. is quickly expanding its locations across Minnesota. Our motto since day one has always been, "Shine the Golden Arches." Since then, we have worked diligently to keep up with the excellence customers expect from McDonald's™ . In doing this, we seek motivated and hard-working individuals to be a part of our team. There's plenty of room to grow within Hyder Invesments and YSH LLC. With hard work and dedication, Crew Members can expand their knowledge of McDonald's™ and apply the skills they learned on the job to anything in life!
